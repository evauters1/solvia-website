Our Story

Solvia Health Services was founded on one conviction: every person deserves dependable, dignified care — and every organization deserves a partner they can count on.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, compassionate, and professional healthcare support services that improve access and quality of care for the individuals, families, and organizations who trust us — especially those underserved by existing systems.

Our Vision

To become a nationally recognized, woman-owned healthcare support company — growing from Metro Chicagoland throughout Illinois and beyond, known for the consistency, excellence, and genuine care we bring to every client we serve.

"We were built for this. Not just to provide a service — but to set a standard."

— Erica Vauters, Founder & CEO

Why We Exist

Every Client Is Someone's Family.

Behind every service request is a real person and a real family navigating a system that was not designed to catch them. Solvia was built for that family. We show up with dignity, consistency, and genuine care — because that is what every person deserves.

Our Founders

EV
Erica Vauters
CEO & Founder

Erica Vauters is the CEO and founder of Solvia Health Services. She built Solvia because she kept seeing the same gap — patients who couldn't get to treatment, specimens that needed to move after hours, families caring for a loved one with no real support. She decided to be the solution.

With a background in healthcare, organizational leadership, community development, and an advanced degree in Christian Leadership and Business, Erica leads Solvia with the conviction that every client deserves care that is personal, consistent, and dignified. When you work with Solvia, you work with her.

WV
Walker Vauters
Co-Founder & Director of Transportation

Walker Vauters is the Co-Founder and Director of Transportation of Solvia Health Services. A certified transport professional, Walker brings the operational experience, logistical discipline, and community trust that Solvia's transportation services are built on.

Together, Erica and Walker lead Solvia as a team with a shared mission — to build a company that treats every client like family, holds every team member to the highest standard, and delivers on its word. Every single time.

What Drives Us

Core Values

Faith

We operate with purpose, humility, and a deep sense of responsibility. Every decision reflects our commitment to doing what is right — for our clients, our team, and our community.

Integrity

We do what we say we will do. Our word is our contract. If we cannot deliver something with full confidence, we will tell you — and find the right solution.

Service

We take the cases others decline. We answer when others have gone home. True service means showing up consistently — not just when it is convenient.

Honor

We treat every resource — time, money, relationships — with the care it deserves. We are stewards of the trust placed in us.

Excellence

Every team member trained. Every shift documented. Every client served at the highest standard. We benchmark above average — because the people we serve deserve nothing less.

Dignity

Every client deserves care that honors who they are. Every team member deserves a workplace that values what they bring. Dignity is non-negotiable at Solvia.
