Patient Transportation

Active Now · Non-emergency ambulatory transport · After-hours coverage available

Reliable, on-time non-emergency transportation for individuals who need to get where they're going — safely and with dignity. Available Monday through Saturday with Sunday emergency response.

  • Scheduled medical appointment transport
  • Same-day transport available
  • Emergency response coverage

Medical Courier & Logistics

Active Now · Specimen transport · Pharmaceutical delivery · STAT & after-hours runs

Clinical-grade courier service with chain-of-custody documentation on every run. We cover the evening and rural gaps other couriers won't — because that's exactly where patients and facilities need us most.

  • Scheduled specimen transport
  • Pharmaceutical & supply delivery
  • STAT same-day courier
  • After-hours emergency courier
  • Weekly recurring route contracts
  • Chain-of-custody documentation

Private Duty Home Support

Active Now · CNA-supervised · Dementia, behavioral health & total dependent care

We take the cases other agencies decline. Every care plan is supervised by a CNA and Nursing Professional. Every caregiver is trained to Solvia's clinical standard before their first shift.

  • Dementia & Alzheimer's care — all stages
  • Behavioral health & IDD support
  • Total dependent care with full PPE
  • Overnight flat-rate coverage

  • LTC insurance invoice support

Mobile Drug Testing

Launching Soon · DOT & non-DOT · Schools, employers, municipalities & DCFS

On-site drug testing that comes to you — fully documented, chain-of-custody compliant, and available after hours. Serving employers, schools, government agencies, and DCFS across Will County and surrounding areas.

  • After-hours drug testing
