Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, compassionate, and strategic healthcare support services that improve access, efficiency, and quality of care in the communities we serve — especially those the system has overlooked.

Our Vision

To become a nationally recognized, woman-owned healthcare support ecosystem — building from courier logistics to patient transportation, workforce staffing, and a training academy serving communities with excellence and dignity.

Leadership

Our Founders

EV
Dr. Erica Vauters
CEO & Founder  ·  51% Owner

Dr. Erica Vauters is a Nursing Professional, Compliance Manager, and the CEO and founder of Solvia Health Services LLC. She brings years of direct clinical experience in dementia care, behavioral health support, and healthcare compliance.

She holds an active CNA credential, HIPAA certification, and a PhD in Christian Leadership and Business. She is a Rotarian, an Easter Seals board member, and a woman who has spent her entire career watching people fall through the cracks of a healthcare system not designed to catch them.

She built Solvia because she kept saying someone should fix this — and then realized that someone was her.

PhD — Christian Leadership & Business Active CNA Nursing Professional HIPAA Certified CPR Certified Rotarian Easter Seals Board
WV
Walker Vauters
Director of Transportation  ·  49% Co-Owner

Walker Vauters is the Director of Transportation and 49% co-owner of Solvia Health Services LLC. A PACE-certified transport professional and community leader, Walker brings operational discipline, community trust, and a servant-leader approach to Solvia's transportation and logistics division.

Together, Erica and Walker operate Solvia as a faith-driven enterprise — one where excellence is the standard, integrity is non-negotiable, and every client served is treated as if they are family.
