Solvia Health Services was founded on a simple truth: every person deserves dependable, dignified care — and every organization deserves a partner they can count on.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, compassionate, and professional healthcare support services that improve access, efficiency, and quality of care for the individuals, families, and organizations who trust us — especially those underserved by existing systems.

Our Vision

To become a nationally recognized, woman-owned healthcare support company — growing from Metro Chicagoland throughout Illinois and beyond, known for the excellence, consistency, and genuine care we bring to every client we serve.

"We were built for this. Not just to provide a service — but to set a standard."

Our Founders

EV
Dr. Erica Vauters
CEO & Founder  ·  51% Owner

Dr. Erica Vauters is a Nursing Professional, Compliance Manager, and the CEO and founder of Solvia Health Services LLC. She brings years of direct clinical experience in dementia care, behavioral health support, and healthcare compliance — combined with deep roots in community leadership and organizational development.

She holds an active CNA credential, HIPAA certification, CPR certification, and a PhD in Christian Leadership and Business. Her career has been defined by one consistent commitment: making sure people get the support they need, when they need it.

She built Solvia because she kept saying someone should fix the gaps in our healthcare system. Then she realized — that someone was her.

PhD — Christian Leadership & Business Active CNA Nursing Professional HIPAA Certified CPR Certified Community Leader
WV
Walker Vauters
Director of Transportation  ·  49% Co-Owner

Walker Vauters is the Director of Transportation and 49% co-owner of Solvia Health Services LLC. A PACE-certified transport professional and community leader, Walker brings operational discipline, logistical expertise, and a genuine servant-leader approach to Solvia's transportation and logistics division.

Together, Erica and Walker lead Solvia as a husband-and-wife founding team — unified in their commitment to building a company that treats every client like family and every team member like a valued professional. Their combined experience in healthcare, operations, and community service is the foundation Solvia is built on.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

These are not marketing words. They are the standards every Solvia team member is hired to uphold and every client can expect to experience.

Faith

We operate with purpose, humility, and a deep sense of responsibility. Every decision we make reflects our commitment to doing what is right — for our clients, our team, and our community.

Integrity

We do what we say we will do. If we cannot deliver something with full confidence and honesty, we will tell you — and we will find the right solution. Our word is our contract.

Service

We take the cases others decline. We answer when others have gone home. True service means showing up consistently — not just when it is convenient.

Honor

We treat every resource — time, money, relationships — with the care and intentionality it deserves. We are stewards of the trust placed in us, and we never take that lightly.

Excellence

Every team member trained. Every shift documented. Every client served at the highest standard. We benchmark above average — because the people we serve deserve nothing less.

Dignity

Every client deserves care that honors who they are. Every team member deserves a workplace that values what they bring. Dignity is non-negotiable at Solvia — for everyone.
