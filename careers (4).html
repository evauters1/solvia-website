Medical Courier Driver

1099 Independent Contractor  ·  Metro Chicagoland & Edgar County  ·  Flexible Hours

Transport medical specimens, pharmaceuticals, and supplies to clinics, labs, and healthcare facilities. This is a real partnership with a growing company that treats its contractors with respect.

What You Will Do

  • Pick up and deliver specimens and medical supplies
  • Document every run with photos and logs
  • Follow chain-of-custody procedures
  • Cover scheduled routes and on-call STAT runs
  • After-hours and weekend runs available

What We Require

  • Valid Illinois driver's license
  • Clean driving record (MVR pulled)
  • Reliable vehicle in good condition
  • Personal auto insurance — commercial use disclosed
  • Smartphone with GPS
  • FBI background check clearance

Compensation

Paid per completed run. After-hours and STAT runs carry premium rates. Weekly direct deposit. You are responsible for your own taxes as a 1099 contractor.

Patient Transportation Driver

1099 Independent Contractor  ·  Metro Chicagoland & Edgar County  ·  Flexible Hours

Transport real people — seniors, individuals with health challenges, and families counting on someone to show up — to medical appointments, dialysis, and hospital discharge. If you have the heart for it, we have the opportunity.

What You Will Do

  • Transport ambulatory clients to medical appointments
  • Recurring dialysis runs (3x per week routes)
  • Hospital discharge transport
  • After-hours and weekend coverage
  • Treat every client with dignity and patience

What We Require

  • Valid Illinois driver's license
  • Clean MVR — no major violations
  • Reliable, clean, comfortable vehicle
  • Personal auto insurance — commercial use disclosed
  • Professional, compassionate demeanor
  • FBI background check clearance

Compensation

Paid per trip. Base rate plus mileage. Dialysis routes offer consistent recurring income. After-hours runs carry premium rates. Weekly direct deposit.

Companion Care Provider

1099 Independent Contractor  ·  Metro Chicagoland  ·  No Clinical License Required

Support seniors and individuals with daily living in their homes. Meal prep, light housekeeping, medication reminders, companionship. No clinical license required — just genuine care, reliability, and a clean background.

  • Meal preparation and light housekeeping
